# PetObject Project Showcase

Check out the project demo here: [PetObject Demo](https://krish-bhalala.github.io/PetObjectWebsite/)

Welcome! This site serves as a detailed showcase for the PetObject Android app, a group project developed for COMP 3350 (Software Engineering) at the University of Manitoba. While our instructor requires us to keep the source code private for now, we’ve put together this site to document our development journey, team contributions, and the technical insights we gained along the way.

### Project Overview
We completed the entire project, from team formation and planning to final implementation over a 14 week period. We are proud to share that our team earned a final grade of 98% on the project. 

It is important to note that our evaluation focused less on the app's specific idea and more on industry standard engineering practices, including:
- Adherence to scalable and rigorous coding standards.
- Effective teamwork and communication.
- Git hygiene and version control workflows.
- Comprehensive design planning.
- Structured code review processes.
- Compliance to Agile Methodlogies.
- Maintainablility of the project.

### Course's Teaching Approach
To mirror real world software environments, our team members were randomly assigned by the instructor. This pushed us to adapt quickly, learn how to collaborate effectively, and manage team dynamics professionally. 

Notably, none of us had prior experience with Android development when we started. We balanced our coursework with self-teaching, proactively helping each other navigate the Android ecosystem to build a functional, polished application.
